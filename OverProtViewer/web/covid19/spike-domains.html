Spike glycoprotein (P0DTC2) – division into domains

Pfam annotation - shitty

Manual annotation

Datasets

Datasets are based on manual annotation, because Pfam annotations are inaccurate (e.g. S1_N contains part of S1_C and S1_RBD).

S1_Y is my invention - it's simply the rest of the protein.

The seed domain is cut from the seed PDB according to the manual annotation. Then DALI server is used to find similar domains. For each domain 3 datasets are produced:

  • This = DALI results against UniProt ID of our protein of interest
  • Similar = DALI results against whole PDB
  • Similar-PDB90 = DALI results against PDB90 (90% non-redundant)

The numbers in the table are: n_domains / n_PDB_entries
